SolKadhi

Description

Solkadhi is a refreshing and flavorful drink from the Konkan region of India. It is made with kokum, coconut milk, and spices, and has a tart and slightly sweet flavor. Solkadhi is often served as an appetizer or as a digestive aid.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 cup kokum (dried mangosteen rind)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the kokum and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Strain the kokum water into a bowl.
  3. In a blender, combine the coconut milk, cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, black pepper, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  4. Add the blended mixture to the kokum water. Stir to combine.
  5. Serve chilled.
